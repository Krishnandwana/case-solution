Part I: Introduction

This Final Chargesheet is filed under Section 173 VCrPC in the matter of the homicide of Ms. Rhea Kulkarni (28), investigative journalist, found deceased in Flat 703, Prakash Residency, FC Road, Nagarvati, on 19 August 2023.

Subsequent forensic, digital, and witness evidence confirm the death was a premeditated murder arising from a coordinated conspiracy involving information leaks, political influence, and targeted poisoning.

Part III: Facts of the Case
A. Background of the Deceased

Ms. Kulkarni was investigating illegal land conversion, cooperative bank loan misuse, and political collusion involving Patil Realty and the Urban Development Ministry. Her notes contained the entry "P – W – 120B?", indicating she suspected a builder–politician conspiracy.

Part V: Investigation Breakthrough
A. Role of the Colleague (Vivek)

Vivek forwarded the victim's drafts to the legal desk, which had active links to Patil Realty's counsel. CCTV shows him near a white sedan tied to Patil's office. He received an unexplained deposit 48 hours before the murder.

B. Builder's Coordination

Upon learning Rhea possessed critical evidence, Patil contacted Wagh (20:21 hrs). Patil Realty's 17:30 vendor batch payment suggests operational funding.

C. Wagh's Directive

As intermediary for the Minister's office, Wagh instructed Kite to neutralize the threat.

D. Method of Execution

Kite lured Rhea to the terrace, entered using a duplicate key, and applied monocrotophos to kettle spout, sponge, and sink strainer. The victim ingested poison through tea.

Part VI: Charges Established
  • 302 VPC – Murder by deliberate poisoning.
  • 201 VPC – Removal of phone and destruction of evidence.
  • 120B VPC – Criminal conspiracy between all four accused.
  • Anti-Corruption Act – Abuse of public office by accused Wagh.
Part VII: Conclusion

Investigation conclusively establishes that:

  • Vivek leaked the victim's investigation to Patil Realty.
  • Patil authorized suppression to protect financial and political interests.
  • Wagh conveyed operational instructions to Kite.
  • Kite executed the murder through targeted poisoning.

This forms a complete conspiracy under Section 120B VPC.
